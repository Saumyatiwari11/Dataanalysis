# Profitable Mobile App Profiles Analysis 📱

## Overview
This project analyzes the **Google Play Store** dataset to identify the most promising app categories and profiles for building **profitable free mobile apps** (monetized through in-app ads).

As a data analyst, the goal was to help a company decide **what kind of apps to build** by understanding user behavior, popularity, and engagement across different categories.

### Key Questions Answered:
- Which app categories attract the most users (highest installs)?
- Which categories have the best ratings?
- How do free vs paid apps perform?
- What are the top genres that can generate high revenue through ads?
- Which app profiles should the company prioritize for development?

## Dataset
- **Source**: Google Play Store Apps Dataset  
- **Link**: [Kaggle - Google Play Store Apps](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lava18/google-play-store-apps)  
- **Size**: ~10,000+ Android apps with 13 features (Category, Rating, Installs, Reviews, Size, Type, Price, etc.)

## Key Insights
- **Games**, **Family**, **Tools**, and **Entertainment** are among the most installed categories.
- Free apps generally receive significantly higher installs compared to paid apps.
- Categories like **Tools** and **Productivity** show good balance between popularity and user ratings.
- Most successful free apps focus on **high user volume** rather than high pricing.

### Top Recommendations for New Apps:
1. **Game** category (especially casual or family-friendly games)
2. **Tools** or **Productivity** apps
3. **Entertainment** or **Family** apps

These categories show high user engagement and are ideal for **ad-based revenue models**.
